1. Smart meal planner

- Drag recipes onto a weekly calendar.
- Auto-generate a grocery list from planned meals.
- High user value, strong retention feature.

2. Pantry and expiry tracker

- Users track ingredients they already have.
- Show “cook this before it expires” suggestions.
- Reduces waste and makes recommendations feel personal.

3. Advanced search and filters

- Filter by prep time, cuisine, diet, allergens, difficulty, ingredient include/exclude.
- Add saved searches like “quick weekday dinners”.

4. Guided cooking mode

- Step-by-step fullscreen mode with built-in timers per step.
- Optional voice controls: next step, repeat, set timer 8 min.

5. Ingredient substitution engine

- “I don’t have buttermilk, what can I use?”
- Add confidence labels: perfect, good, emergency substitute.

6. Recipe scaling and unit conversion

- Scale servings up/down cleanly.
- Convert metric/imperial automatically.

7. Grocery list intelligence

- Merge duplicate items from multiple recipes.
- Group by category (produce, dairy, spices).
- Optional “already in pantry” exclusion.

8. Recipe import assistant

- Import from URL, photo, or pasted text.
- Parse ingredients/steps automatically for quick editing.

9. Nutrition and allergen badges

- Estimated macros per serving.
- Visual allergen tags and dietary flags (vegan, gluten-free, nut-free).

10. Social layer

- Public profiles, follow cooks, recipe collections.
- “Cooked this” reviews with photos.

11. Admin quality tools

- Duplicate recipe detection.
- Moderation queue for reported content.
- Health dashboard: most searched, abandoned recipes, top engagement.

12. Seasonal and contextual suggestions

- Summer BBQ, winter soups, holiday bundles.
- “What to cook tonight” based on weather and time available.
